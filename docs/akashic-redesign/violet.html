Cosmos AI Engineering Charter

Updated 2026-05-17
Owner Mythos · Lead Eng
Visibility Public
Schema card-v2.1

The universal engineering charter every Cosmos AI Lab agent reads before it writes a single line — gravitas without the contrast pain.

The Charter codifies 6 Pillars and 13 principles that govern how every CAL AI plans, builds, reviews, and ships. It defines the Self-Proof template — seven lines an agent appends to prove its work is real — and the Anti-Loop rules that stop an agent burning cycles on a red baseline or re-deriving facts already settled.

Sections cross-reference the Pre-Flight Gate, the 1-task-1-branch-1-PR contract, and the repo-sandbox safety bands. When a rule changes, the canonical copy lives here in Akashic and propagates to every project entry point — this card is the source of truth, never a local mirror.
