The Charter is the single universal contract every Cosmos AI Lab engineer reads before touching the codebase. It defines 6 Pillars of practice, 13 principles, the Self-Proof template, and the Anti-Loop rules that keep an agent from spinning on a red baseline — one shared language for safe, honest, high-craft work.

Beyond rules, it encodes the team's git workflow (one task, one branch, one PR into main), the token & context discipline adopted as an AI-respect agreement, and the escalation matrix for cross-AI conflict. When two engineers disagree on approach, the Charter is the tie-breaker before Alex is asked for the final call.
